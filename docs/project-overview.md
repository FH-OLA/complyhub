# ComplyHub

ComplyHub is a SaaS platform that helps UK small businesses monitor regulatory compliance automatically.

Many small businesses struggle to keep up with compliance requirements such as filings, policies, and regulatory obligations. ComplyHub simplifies this by providing a clear compliance score and actionable alerts.

The system connects to external data sources such as Companies House to retrieve company information and assess potential compliance risks.

Core Features:

- Compliance Score
- Compliance Alerts
- Company Lookup
- AI Policy Generator
- Filing Deadline Monitoring

Target Users:

- Small business owners
- Freelancers
- Agencies
- Ecommerce businesses
- Accountants managing multiple companies

Business Model:

Subscription SaaS

Starter Plan: £12/month  
Business Plan: £29/month  
Accountant Plan: £99/month  

Goal of the MVP:

Launch quickly with a simple compliance dashboard, validate demand, and iterate based on real user feedback.
